# Hearts of Iron IV: Capitulation Messages Popup

This mod replaces vanilla HOI4's generic news events that are triggered when a country capitulates during a war with a popup GUI, paired with a capitulation message that matches the capitulated nation. At the moment, it is tailored to the historical HOI4 vanilla experience, and the messages are mainly catered to such scenarios.

This GUI is made in the style of Equestria at War that I implemented for the Equestria at War development team.
